=========
Citations
=========

When using FinalCif or any of the software it integrates with, please cite the appropriate
references listed below.


FinalCif
--------

* D. Kratzert, *FinalCif*, https://dkratzert.de/finalcif.html.


CheckCIF and Validation
-----------------------

**IUCr CheckCIF** (online validation service)

* IUCr CheckCIF/PLATON service: https://checkcif.iucr.org/

**PLATON** (offline structure validation, including SQUEEZE)

* A. L. Spek, *Acta Cryst.* **2009**, *D65*, 148–155, doi:`10.1107/S090744490804362X <https://doi.org/10.1107/S090744490804362X>`_.
* A. L. Spek, *Acta Cryst.* **2015**, *C71*, 9–18, doi:`10.1107/S2053229614024929 <https://doi.org/10.1107/S2053229614024929>`_ (SQUEEZE).
* PLATON homepage: https://www.platonsoft.nl/platon/


Data Collection and Integration
--------------------------------

**SAINT** (Bruker data integration)

* Bruker, *SAINT*, Bruker AXS Inc., Madison, Wisconsin, USA.

**CrysAlisPro** (Rigaku data collection and reduction)

* *CrysAlisPro*, Rigaku OD, Yarnton, Oxfordshire, UK.

**XDS** (data processing for rotation images)

* W. Kabsch, *Acta Cryst.* **2010**, *D66*, 125–132, doi:`10.1107/S0907444909047337 <https://doi.org/10.1107/S0907444909047337>`_.


Absorption Correction
---------------------

**SADABS / TWINABS** (Bruker area-detector absorption correction)

* L. Krause, R. Herbst-Irmer, G. M. Sheldrick, D. Stalke, *J. Appl. Cryst.* **2015**, *48*, 3–10, doi:`10.1107/S1600576714022985 <https://doi.org/10.1107/S1600576714022985>`_.

**scale3abspack** (Oxford Diffraction absorption correction)

* Oxford Diffraction Ltd., *scale3abspack*, Abingdon, Oxford (U.K.) 2005.

**SORTAV** (multi-scan absorption correction)

* R. H. Blessing, *Cryst. Rev.* **1987**, *1*, 3–58, doi:`10.1080/08893118708081678 <https://doi.org/10.1080/08893118708081678>`_.

**X-RED** (STOE absorption correction)

* Stoe & Cie, *X-RED*, Stoe & Cie, Darmstadt, Germany.


Structure Solution
------------------

**SHELXT** (structure solution by intrinsic phasing)

* G. M. Sheldrick, *Acta Cryst.* **2015**, *A71*, 3–8, doi:`10.1107/S2053273314026370 <https://doi.org/10.1107/S2053273314026370>`_.

**SHELXS** (structure solution by Patterson and direct methods)

* G. M. Sheldrick, *Acta Cryst.* **2008**, *A64*, 112–122, doi:`10.1107/S0108767307043930 <https://doi.org/10.1107/S0108767307043930>`_.

**SHELXD** (structure solution for large structures and macromolecules)

* I. Usón, G. M. Sheldrick, *Acta Cryst.* **2018**, *D74*, 106–116, doi:`10.1107/S2059798317015121 <https://doi.org/10.1107/S2059798317015121>`_.

**NoSpherA2** (non-spherical atom refinement)

* F. Kleemiss, O. V. Dolomanov, M. Bodensteiner, N. Peyerimhoff, L. Midgley, L. J. Bourhis, A. Genoni,
